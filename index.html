Welcome to Betta Love



This website is dedicated to Betta enthusiats or anyone looking into purchasing and caring for a Betta. Betta are some of the most beautiful freshwater fish you can purchase.
from their fancy fins to their stunning colors, its hard to not fall in love with Betta fish. Betta fish are great beginner fish and are not too difficult to care for.
Feel free to browse our site and learn about how to take care of your future Betta!
